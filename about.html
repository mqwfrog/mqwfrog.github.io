About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Software, Shandong University, China, under the supervision of Professor Lizhen Cui. I received the Master’s degree in the School of Software, Shandong University, China in 2020, and the Bachelor’s degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, China in 2017.

My research interests mainly lie in representation learning, unsupervised learning, and time series modeling.
